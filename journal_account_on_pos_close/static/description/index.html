With this module, the income account used on the pos close become the pos journal account instead of the product account.

Thus, it makes it possible to use the default revenue account configured in the point of sale journal for matching when closing a point of sale session.

How does it work ?

You have to start by creating a specific article on which a category of articles links to an account.

Then you have to create a point of sale for which the sales journal is configured to break down the sale in question.

The accounting document is thus on the account of the journal and not that of the category.

If an account is set up on the article, then the newspaper account must also be taken over.

When creating an invoice via the point of sale, we do not use the account of the journal, but that of the category or the article, unless nothing is entered in the category or the article, then it will be necessary to take again the account of the newspaper.


    Features :
  • Replaced product account with point of sale journal account
  • Use default product account when lettering
  • Addition of accounting document to the journal account
  • Consideration of the category or item account when creating an invoice
